# Purpose

- Goal: automate testing of payment scenarios for the contact chip card reader.

# Current Process

- Testers currently insert and remove the card manually.

# Target State

- Tests for the contact chip card reader are started and executed automatically in the pipeline without participation of a manual tester.
- All manual actions are not performed.

# Scope and Industry

- Industry: manufacturer of payment terminals for different countries (150+).

# Mandatory Supported Scenarios

- The system must support at least all EMV L3 and local certification scenarios.
